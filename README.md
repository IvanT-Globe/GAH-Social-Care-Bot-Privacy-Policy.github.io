# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 22, 2026
**Last Updated:** May 22, 2026

Globe Telecom, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") operates the application GAH Social Care Bot (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use the App or when the App processes data from Facebook Pages you administer.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information from Facebook / Meta Platforms

When you connect a Facebook Page to the App, we may receive the following information through the Meta Graph API and Webhooks:

- Facebook Page ID, name, and category
- Posts published to the connected Page, including post ID, message text, attachments, and timestamps
- Comments on the connected Page's posts, including comment ID, message text, author name, author ID, parent comment ID (if any), and timestamps
- Reactions and engagement metadata associated with posts and comments
- Page access tokens issued by Meta to authorize API access

We do **not** collect information from Facebook users who interact with your Page beyond what is publicly available or what the Page administrator has authorized us to access.

### 1.2 Information You Provide

If you create an account or contact us, we may collect:

- Name and email address
- Login credentials (passwords are stored hashed)
- Any other information you voluntarily submit

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

- IP address and approximate location
- Browser type, device type, and operating system
- Log data (request timestamps, pages accessed, errors)
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 7)

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the App
- Receive and process webhook events from Meta in real time
- Display comments, posts, and analytics back to authorized users
- Authenticate users and secure access to the App
- Communicate with you about service updates, security alerts, and support
- Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, and security incidents
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **not** sell your personal information or the personal information of users who interact with your Pages.

## 3. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

- **Performance of a contract** — to provide the App
- **Legitimate interests** — to operate, improve, and secure the App
- **Consent** — where you have explicitly consented
- **Legal obligation** — where required by applicable law

## 4. How We Share Information

We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service providers** — with vendors who help us operate the App (hosting, monitoring, analytics), bound by confidentiality obligations
- **Legal requirements** — when required by law, subpoena, or legitimate government request
- **Business transfers** — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with prior notice
- **With your consent** — when you authorize us to share

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy:

- Page content (posts, comments) retained while your Page is connected to the App
- Account information retained while your account is active
- Log data retained for up to 365 days
- Information may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes

You may request earlier deletion as described in Section 6.

## 6. Data Deletion and Your Rights

### 6.1 How to Request Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by:

- Disconnecting your Facebook Page from the App through the App's settings, which will trigger deletion of all data associated with that Page within [30] days
- Emailing us at **ivan.tarun@globe.com.ph** with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
- Submitting a request through Facebook's Settings → Apps and Websites, which will trigger our Data Deletion Callback

We will confirm the deletion within a reasonable timeframe.

### 6.2 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- Delete your personal information
- Restrict or object to certain processing
- Data portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format)
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **ivan.tarun@globe.com.ph**.

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

The App may use cookies and similar technologies to authenticate sessions, remember preferences, and analyze usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect functionality.

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Access controls and audit logging
- Regular security reviews

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your country of residence, including Philippines. Where required by law, we use appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) for cross-border transfers.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.
